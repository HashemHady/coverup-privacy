Privacy Policy for Cover Up

Last Updated: October 21, 2025

Summary: Cover Up does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected.

1. Introduction

Cover Up ("we", "our", or "the app") is a privacy-focused screen overlay application for Android devices. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Cover Up does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device information (device ID, IP address, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Screen content or screenshots
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Crash reports
  • Any other user data

3. Data Storage

Cover Up stores the following preferences locally on your device only:

  • Overlay position and size
  • Opacity settings
  • Auto-hide timer settings
  • Overlay running state

This data is stored using Android's SharedPreferences and never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any servers or third parties.

4. Permissions

Cover Up requires the following Android permissions:

  • Display over other apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW): Required to show the overlay on top of other applications. This is the core functionality of the app.
  • Foreground service: Required to keep the overlay running while the app is in the background.

These permissions are used solely for the app's functionality and do not enable any data collection.

5. Third-Party Services

Cover Up does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. No data is shared with third parties because no data is collected in the first place.

6. Children's Privacy

Cover Up does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Your Rights

Since Cover Up does not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. All settings are stored locally on your device and can be reset by uninstalling the app.

9. Data Security

While Cover Up does not collect any data, we have implemented security measures in the app's design:

  • All preferences are stored locally using Android's secure storage
  • No network connections are made
  • No data is transmitted
  • Code is obfuscated in release builds

10. Open Source

Cover Up is committed to transparency. Users can verify our privacy claims by reviewing the source code (if made available) or examining the app's network activity, which will show zero data transmission.
